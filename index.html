The game

Hello fellow nature-lover! On this site you can find the description of the new game 're->Cycle!'

This is a game that is based on protecting the environment and recycling rubbish. Your character’s goal is to save the forest from pollution and collect as much trash before the timer runs out. This game is especially for children and it encourages them to recycle and be responsible for their actions while interacting with nature. And remember-you have to be quick, don’t let the nasty stuff spread!
